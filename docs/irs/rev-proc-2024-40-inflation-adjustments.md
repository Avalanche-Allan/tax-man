# Revenue Procedure 2024-40 -- 2025 Inflation Adjustments

> **Source:** [IRS Revenue Procedure 2024-40](https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-drop/rp-24-40.pdf)

> **Average Inflation Adjustment:** Approximately 2.8% increase over 2024

## 2. Standard Deduction (2025)

### Base Standard Deduction (Pre-OBBBA / Rev. Proc. 2024-40)

| Filing Status | Rev. Proc. 2024-40 Amount | OBBBA-Adjusted Amount |
|---|---|---|
| Single | $15,000 | **$15,750** |
| Married Filing Jointly (MFJ) | $30,000 | **$31,500** |
| Married Filing Separately (MFS) | $15,000 | **$15,750** |
| Head of Household (HOH) | $22,500 | **$23,625** |
| Qualifying Surviving Spouse (QSS) | $30,000 | **$31,500** |

> OBBBA Section 101 increased the standard deduction above the Rev. Proc. 2024-40 amounts for 2025.

### Additional Standard Deduction (Age 65+ or Blind)

| Condition | Married (MFJ/MFS) | Unmarried (Single/HOH) |
|---|---|---|
| Age 65 or older | $1,600 per person | $2,000 |
| Blind | $1,600 per person | $2,000 |
| Both 65+ and blind | $3,200 per person | $4,000 |

### OBBBA Senior Bonus Deduction (2025-2028)

An additional **$6,000** deduction for individuals age 65 and older:
- Single filers: Income limit $75,000 (phases out above)
- MFJ filers: Income limit $150,000 (phases out above)
- MFS filers: **Not eligible**

## 4. Long-Term Capital Gains Tax Brackets (2025)

Applies to assets held more than one year. These thresholds are based on **taxable income**.

| Rate | Single | MFJ / QSS | MFS | HOH |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0% | $0 -- $48,350 | $0 -- $96,700 | $0 -- $48,350 | $0 -- $64,750 |
| 15% | $48,351 -- $533,400 | $96,701 -- $600,050 | $48,351 -- $300,000 | $64,751 -- $566,700 |
| 20% | Over $533,400 | Over $600,050 | Over $300,000 | Over $566,700 |

> **Additional 3.8% Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT)** applies when modified AGI exceeds $200,000 (Single/HOH), $250,000 (MFJ/QSS), or $125,000 (MFS). The NIIT thresholds are NOT inflation-adjusted.

## 5. Foreign Earned Income Exclusion (FEIE) -- IRC Section 911

| Parameter | 2025 Amount |
|---|---|
| Maximum foreign earned income exclusion | **$130,000** |
| Housing exclusion base amount (16% of FEIE) | $20,800 |
| Housing exclusion default maximum (30% of FEIE) | $39,000 |

> The FEIE only excludes income from **income tax**, not from self-employment tax. High-cost locality adjustments may increase the housing maximum above $39,000.

## 6. Qualified Business Income (QBI) Deduction -- IRC Section 199A

| Parameter | Amount |
|---|---|
| QBI deduction rate | 20% of qualified business income |
| Taxable income threshold (MFJ) | **$394,600** |
| Taxable income threshold (Single/HOH/MFS) | **$197,300** |
| Phase-out range (MFJ) | $394,600 -- $494,600 ($100,000 range) |
| Phase-out range (other filers) | $197,300 -- $247,300 ($50,000 range) |

Above the threshold, the deduction may be limited based on:
- **W-2 wages** paid by the qualified trade or business
- **Unadjusted basis** of qualified property (UBIA)
- **Specified service trade or business (SSTB)** limitations

> OBBBA made Section 199A permanent (it was previously set to expire after 2025).
